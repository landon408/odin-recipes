Description

This a recipe for the classic pizza. So simple but so good. With some bread, chesse, tomato sauce, and pepperoni, you can feed the entire family!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500F
  2. Make sauce
  3. Roll Dough
  4. Add Toppings
  5. Cook Pizza
  6. Eat!
